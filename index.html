Our Programs

Engaging programming designed for every stage of life — from musical exploration with toddlers to therapeutic sessions with seniors.

Pop Music Party

A high-energy dance party featuring age-appropriate music from popular culture. Children move, groove, and celebrate the joy of music together.

Music on the Farm

Make adorable animal crafts while singing along with farm-themed songs. Children learn about animals through rhythm, movement, and creativity.

Music with Bluey

Family portraits, the Bluey theme song, magic xylophone adventures, and keepy uppey! A beloved character-themed musical experience.

Instrument Petting Zoo

Touch and play a variety of string, percussion, and bell instruments. Children explore sounds, textures, and the basics of music-making.

Music and Movement

Clapping, stomping, swaying, and dancing to favorite songs. Children develop motor skills and rhythm awareness through joyful movement.

Music Story Time

Read a musical story together, then create a live soundtrack using instruments and voices. Stories come alive through collaborative music-making.

Music Mindfulness

Breathing exercises, focus techniques, and sound appreciation. Children learn to listen deeply and find calm through musical awareness.

Music Makers

Make instruments to take home — percussion shakers, rubber band guitars, bell sticks, and kazoos. Hands-on crafting meets musical exploration.

Instrument Scavenger Hunt

Hunt for hidden instruments around the space, learn their unique sounds, then practice playing together as a group orchestra.

Music and Math

Counting beats, clapping patterns, and discovering rhythm through numbers. Music + Math = FUN! STEAM learning through song.

Music and Language

Learn new words through rhyming songs, repeating word games, and call-and-response activities. Building vocabulary through music.

Junkyard Orchestra

Transform recycled materials into working musical instruments. Children learn the science of sound while building something they can actually play. Environmental sustainability meets creative music-making.

Explore pitch, vibration, and frequency through hands-on experiments. Discover how sound waves work, why different instruments sound different, and the physics behind music. STEAM at its finest.

Music Olympics

Musical chairs, freeze dance, rhythmic relay races, and more — all for medals! Children compete in fun, music-based challenges that build listening skills and coordination.

Create original soundtracks for favorite cartoon clips. Children learn how music creates mood, tension, and humor while composing their own accompaniment.

Puppet Musical Theater

Create simple puppets, then put on a musical performance complete with songs and sound effects. Storytelling, crafting, and music combine in one creative experience.

Drum Circle Jam

Explore percussion instruments and rhythms from various cultures around the world. Children learn about global musical traditions while playing together as a drum circle.

Video Game Music

Explore the iconic soundtracks of Minecraft, MarioKart, Plants vs. Zombies, and more. Discover how composers create memorable game music and try composing your own.

Disney Music Mania

Sing and dance to beloved Disney songs from classic and contemporary films. A high-energy celebration of the music that defines childhood magic.

Superhero Soundtrack

World Explorer's Melodies

Travel the globe through traditional music from different countries and cultures. Children learn about instruments, rhythms, and songs from around the world.

Music Memory Games

Match instruments to their sounds, identify famous composers by their music, and test musical knowledge through fun competitive games.

Sound Sculptures

Build three-dimensional sculptures that actually create sounds. Art meets engineering meets music in this creative STEAM workshop.

Musical Sci-Fi

Journey through space with music and sound effects. Create alien soundscapes, compose spaceship themes, and explore how sci-fi films use music to build other worlds.

STEAM Programs

Music meets science

$250/session or $800 for 4-session series

STEAM

Excavation Orchestra

Children "excavate" materials from a dig site and build working instruments from their finds — PVC tubes, containers, rubber bands, sticks, coconut shells. Like paleontologists piecing together dinosaurs, kids piece together an orchestra, learning sound science along the way. The session culminates in a group performance of a "prehistoric symphony."

60 min · Ages 6-11 · $250

STEAM

Dino Sound Lab

How did Jurassic Park create the T-Rex roar? What did a Parasaurolophus — the "trombone dinosaur" — actually sound like? Kids explore where paleontology meets acoustics, build PVC pipe "crests" demonstrating how tube length changes pitch, then create their own dinosaur vocalizations using layered sounds.

60 min · Ages 8-12 · $250

STEAM

Prehistoric Instrument Petting Zoo

Each station represents a geological era with different instruments. Kids explore real instruments alongside prehistoric-inspired creations — PVC pan pipes ("bone flutes"), bucket drums ("cave percussion"), rubber-band box guitars. Passport system where children earn fossil stamps at each station.

60 min drop-in · Ages 3-12 · $250

STEAM

Fossil Record Escape Room

Teams decode "fossilized" musical notation, solve rhythm puzzles embedded in geological layers, identify dinosaur species by their reconstructed "calls," and crack a final combination using note values. Clues can be hidden in library stacks using call numbers from the science section.

45-60 min · Ages 12-18 · $250

STEAM

Jurassic Soundtrack Lab

Teens analyze and remix iconic movie scores — the Jurassic Park theme by John Williams is instantly recognizable. Using GarageBand or similar software, they create original soundtracks for dinosaur documentary footage, learning how film composers use orchestration, dynamics, and leitmotifs.

60-75 min · Ages 13-18 · $250

4-Session Series

Fossil Strum Ukulele

Multi-week ukulele course where each session "unearths" a new chord and skill. Session 1 excavates the C chord ("the fossil everyone finds first"), Session 2 digs up G and Am, building toward a group performance of themed songs. The paleontology metaphor makes progression feel like discovery.

4 sessions · Ages 13+ · $800
